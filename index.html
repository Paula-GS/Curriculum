About myself

I am Paula González Silva, a full-stack Python developer who also has a background in linguistics and translation, I speak two languages professionally: Portuguese and English and three other languages at a basic level. I like to learn and be in constant intellectual and professional development, so I am looking for a job where I can grow and expand my knowledge and programming skills, especially in the front-end area.
Within my work experience, I have developed the skills of dealing with crises and therefore mitigating a situation, for example, an annoyed customer in retail or leading work teams to carry out a task effectively and efficiently.
Another skill I have is the ability to research and find an answer to some inquiries or some missing information. I can also organize the information so it's easily accessible to everyone who occupies it regularly. I'm not afraid to ask if I have any doubts because it's the only way to learn and step forward.
