About myself

Translator and Linguist from the University of Santiago de Chile, who has an extensive experience in commercial, administrative and industrial areas. She has worked mainly in the legal and customer service areas. Contact interpreting, liaison interpreting, simultaneous to and from English and Portuguese. Advanced Office and file or document management, research capability, problem-solving.
I have a facility for languages, art and history; I like research and being in constant learning for the intellectual, personal and professional growth of myself and my students. I am currently looking for opportunities to grow in the area of Front-end and programming, I have availability to travel and work online. My work experience has allowed me to develop in the area of sales and national and foreign customer service, as well as conflict resolution and crisis management, I have an ability for teamwork, and I have successfully led work groups, events organization and tourist guides completing tasks in time and efficiency.
As a freelancer, I have worked as a translator and interpreter in various fields such as legal, medical and industrial. In other areas, I have excelled in my self-taught skills such as confectionery and gastronomy and the sale of the products and making garments such as the elaboration of protective suits for medical personnel during the COVID-19 pandemic.
